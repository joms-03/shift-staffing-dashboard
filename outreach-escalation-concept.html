How the escalation works

1

CallReach out by phone first — it converts best, especially close to shift start.

2

RemindNo answer? Send a quick text/push nudge instead of calling again right away.

3

Recommend overfillStill silent as the shift gets close? We flag the shift for a backup overfill slot so the business isn't left short.

4

DismissNo response after all attempts — clear them from the queue so no one keeps chasing a dead lead.
